GTM Engineer Skills is an open-source collection of agent skills for go-to-market engineering. Each skill is a focused `SKILL.md` prompt that you install once into Claude Code or Codex, then invoke by name to automate a specific GTM workflow — from researching brand DNA all the way through to making your website visible to AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

## Why GTM engineering needs AI-native tools

The web is shifting from human-first to AI-first discovery. ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews now answer millions of queries that used to drive search clicks. AI engines don't browse like humans — they extract structured data, look for direct answers, and decide in milliseconds whether content is worth citing.

GTM Engineer Skills gives your team the workflows to be visible in that new environment, grounded in peer-reviewed research (GEO paper, KDD 2024) and large-scale industry studies.
